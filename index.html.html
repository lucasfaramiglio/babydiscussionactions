Discuss the questions

Baby Development
Discussion Topics
  • At what age do most babies start to grasp objects with their hands? What are some safe things parents can give babies to hold?
  • Why do you think babies giggle so much? What makes children in your culture laugh the most?
  • When babies babble, are they trying to communicate? How should parents respond to baby sounds?
  • In your country, how long do mothers typically breastfeed their babies? What are the cultural attitudes toward breastfeeding in public?
  • What remedies do people in your culture use when babies are teething? Are there any traditional methods?
  • When babies start to crawl, how should parents prepare their homes? What are the biggest safety concerns?
  • How important is it for parents to cuddle with their babies? Do fathers and mothers show affection differently?
  • Once a baby can sit up independently, what new activities become possible? How does this change daily routines?
  • What's the difference between when a baby starts to toddle versus when they can walk confidently? What should parents expect?
  • How many hours should babies sleep each day? What do parents in your country do when babies won't sleep through the night?
  • Is it exciting when a baby first learns to roll over? What other physical milestones do parents eagerly wait for?
  • A baby's first smile is considered very special. Do you think babies smile because they're happy, or is it just a reflex?
